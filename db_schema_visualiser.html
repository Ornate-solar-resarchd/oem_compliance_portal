id=1 only. Controls approval thresholds per PO category (t1, t2, auto, always_full).
Seeded Config

FINISHED_GOODS → t1: ₹10L, t2: ₹50L
RAW_MATERIAL → t1: ₹10L, t2: ₹50L
OPEX → t1: ₹5L, t2: ₹25L
CONSUMABLES → auto: ₹50K, t1: ₹2L

Status Flows — at a glance
Purchase Order (purchase_orders.status)
DRAFT→ PENDING_APPROVAL→ APPROVED→ ORDERED→ PARTIALLY_RECEIVED→ RECEIVED→ CLOSED | REJECTED CANCELLED
LC Master (lc_master.status)
APPLIED→ ISSUED→ AMENDED→ NEGOTIATED→ CLOSED | EXPIRED
Contract (contracts.status)
DRAFT→ INTERNALLY_APPROVED→ SENT_TO_SUPPLIER→ EXECUTED | TERMINATED
Customs (customs.status)
PENDING→ DUTY_PAID→ OOC→ DELIVERED
LC Amendment (lc_amendments.status)
DRAFT→ PENDING_BANK→ BANK_CONFIRMED→ BENEFICIARY_ACK→ COMPLETED | REJECTED
Role Hierarchy (users.role)
VIEWER< WAREHOUSE< LOGISTICS< ACCOUNTS< PROC_MGR< PROC_HEAD< CFO< DIRECTOR< ADMIN
